================================================================================
                         CORE ALGORITHMS IMPLEMENTED
================================================================================

ALGORITHM #1: INTERLEAVED DEPARTMENT SEATING
File: SeatingService.java - interleaveByDepartment()
Purpose: Prevent copying by mixing students from different departments

Steps:
  1. Group students by department
  2. Interleave round-robin: CSE1, ECE1, MECH1, CSE2, ECE2...
  3. Assign to halls sequentially (row/column)
  4. Generate seat labels: R{row}C{column}

Result: Students next to each other from DIFFERENT departments

---

ALGORITHM #2: LOAD-BALANCED INVIGILATOR ALLOCATION
File: InvigilatorAllocationService.java - allocateInvigilators()
Purpose: Fair duty rotation with department conflict avoidance

Steps:
  1. Sort invigilators by assigned_count (ascending)
  2. Prefer invigilators NOT from exam's department
  3. Round-robin assign to halls
  4. Increment assigned_count after each assignment

Result: Load-balanced, fair, no same-department conflicts
